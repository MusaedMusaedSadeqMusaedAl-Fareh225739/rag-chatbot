HALIFAX ACTIVITIES GUIDE

Welcome to Halifax, Nova Scotia! Here are the exciting activities available during your visit.

KAYAK TOUR
The kayak tour starts in Blue Rocks, a picturesque fishing village. You'll paddle through calm waters surrounded by colorful fishing shacks and lobster boats. The tour takes approximately 2-3 hours and is suitable for beginners. We recommend wearing sporty clothes, sturdy shoes, and bringing a rain jacket.

WALKING TOUR
The walking tour in Halifax takes you along Citadel Hill, offering panoramic views of the city and harbor. You'll learn about the city's rich maritime history and visit historic sites. Duration: approximately 2 hours.

LOBSTER TOUR
Experience the authentic Maritime lobster fishing industry! You'll tour Portland's lobster fishery, learn about sustainable fishing practices, and enjoy a fresh lobster lunch at a local restaurant.

BREWERY TOUR
The Old Port Walk, Talk and Taste Brewery tour combines Halifax's history with local craft beer. Visit three local breweries, sample unique Maritime ales, and learn about the brewing process.

SPIRITS TASTING
Visit Ironworks Distillery for a spirits tasting experience. Sample locally crafted rum, vodka, and fruit liqueurs while learning about the distillation process.

DUNE TOUR
Adventure awaits on our dune tour in off-road vehicles! Explore the stunning coastal sand dunes while your expert guide shares stories about the local ecosystem.

SHUTTLE SERVICE
In the afternoon, shuttles are available every 30 minutes from the main pier to downtown Halifax. The last shuttle returns at 6:00 PM.
