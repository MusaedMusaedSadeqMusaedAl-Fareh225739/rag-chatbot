PACKING LIST & TRAVEL ESSENTIALS

Prepare for your journey with this comprehensive packing guide.

ESSENTIAL DOCUMENTS
- Passport (valid for at least 6 months beyond travel dates)
- ESTA (for US entry) or eTA (for Canada entry)
- Health insurance card
- Printed copies of booking confirmations
- Emergency contact information

CLOTHING RECOMMENDATIONS

Daytime Activities:
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Casual resort wear
- Light layers for varying temperatures
- Swimwear for pool and beach excursions
- Sun hat and sunglasses

Evening Events:
- Smart casual attire for most dinners
- Dress code for New York highlight evening: Glitz & Glamor
- Formal wear for Captain's Dinner

Excursion Gear:
- Sturdy hiking shoes for walking tours
- Rain jacket (waterproof)
- Sporty clothes for active excursions

CARRY-ON LUGGAGE RESTRICTIONS
For air travel portions:
- Liquids: each container must be 100 ml or less (≤ 100 ml)
- Total liquid allowance: 1 liter maximum (≤ 1 L)
- All liquids must fit in a clear, resealable plastic bag
- Medications may exceed limits with proper documentation

ELECTRONICS
- Phone and charger
- Camera
- Universal power adapter (European outlets on ship)
- Portable battery pack

HEALTH & COMFORT
- Prescription medications (in original containers)
- Seasickness remedies if needed
- Sunscreen (SPF 30+)
- Basic first aid items
- Hand sanitizer

TRAVEL TIPS
- Pack a small day bag for excursions
- Leave valuable jewelry at home
- Bring a reusable water bottle
- Pack an extra outfit in your carry-on in case of luggage delays
