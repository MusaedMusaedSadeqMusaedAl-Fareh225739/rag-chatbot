GUEST WIFI CONNECTION GUIDE

Welcome aboard! Stay connected with our complimentary guest WiFi service.

CONNECTING YOUR DEVICE

For Android Smartphones:
1. Open Settings on your device
2. Tap on WLAN (or WiFi)
3. Select "Guest WiFi" from the available networks
4. Wait for the connection to establish
5. Open your browser - you may be redirected to a login page
6. Accept the terms and conditions

For iOS Devices (iPhone/iPad):
1. Open Settings
2. Tap WiFi
3. IMPORTANT: Before connecting, deactivate Private WLAN Address
   - Tap the (i) icon next to the network name
   - Toggle OFF "Private Wi-Fi Address"
4. Select "Guest WiFi" from the network list
5. Accept the terms when prompted

For Laptops (Windows/Mac):
1. Click the WiFi icon in your taskbar/menu bar
2. Select "Guest WiFi"
3. Open your browser to complete registration

NETWORK DETAILS
Network Name (SSID): Guest WiFi
Password: Not required (open network)
Coverage: All public areas and cabins

TROUBLESHOOTING
- If you cannot connect, try forgetting the network and reconnecting
- Restart your device if connection issues persist
- Visit the Guest Services desk on Deck 5 for assistance

USAGE GUIDELINES
- Streaming services may have limited bandwidth
- VPN connections may experience slower speeds
- For business needs, premium WiFi packages are available at Guest Services
