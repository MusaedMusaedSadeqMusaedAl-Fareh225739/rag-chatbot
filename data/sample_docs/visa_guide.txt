VISA & ENTRY REQUIREMENTS GUIDE

Important information about travel documentation for your journey.

CANADA - Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA)

What is an eTA?
An Electronic Travel Authorization is required for visa-exempt foreign nationals traveling to Canada by air.

Cost: 7 CAD (Canadian Dollars)

Application Website: https://www.canada.ca/eta

Required Documents:
- Valid passport
- Credit or debit card for payment
- Email address for confirmation

Processing Time:
Most applications are approved within minutes, but some may take several days.

Validity:
An approved eTA is valid for 5 years or until your passport expires.

UNITED STATES - ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization)

What is ESTA?
ESTA is required for citizens of Visa Waiver Program countries traveling to the United States.

Important: Apply at least 72 hours before departure

Application Steps:
1. Visit the official ESTA website: https://esta.cbp.dhs.gov
2. Complete the online application form
3. Pay the processing fee
4. Submit your application
5. After payment, you'll receive an approval email (usually within minutes to 72 hours)

Required Information:
- Passport details
- Travel itinerary
- Contact information in the US
- Employment information

Validity:
ESTA approval is valid for 2 years or until your passport expires.

GENERAL TRAVEL DOCUMENT CHECKLIST

Before departure, ensure you have:
- Passport (check expiration date!)
- ESTA or eTA confirmation (printed or saved on phone)
- Travel insurance documents
- Cruise booking confirmation
- Flight tickets

IMPORTANT REMINDERS
- Check passport validity: most countries require 6 months validity
- Apply for ESTA/eTA well in advance
- Keep digital and printed copies of all documents
- Register with your country's embassy travel program
